Fintech Services

Travel Services

BBPS Services

BBPS allows clients to pay a wide range of bills like utility bills, DTH, service bills, gas bills, water bills, electricity bills and more. It acts as a one-stop solution for various bill payments.

AEPS Servies

AEPS is an easy-to-use, safe payment system in India that enables users to make transactions using with their Aadhaar number and biometric identification.

DMT Services

DMT is the electronic transfer of cash within a single country. This services are mostly popular in places where regular banking services are difficult to obtain, as well as in areas without banks or with  underbanked population.

Recharge Services

Recharge services refer to the process of adding credit to various accounts or services, typically in the context of mobile phones, DTH services and other prepaid utilities.
